How it works

Three layers. One path.

📝
1. Learn words
Each ayah is broken into individual words. Learn the Arabic, transliteration, and meaning of every word.
🔗
2. Build ayahs
Once you know every word, prove you can identify and recite the full ayah. Understanding before memorization.
📖
3. Chain surahs
When all ayahs are learned, chain them together into full surahs. Then move to the next one.
The science

Spaced repetition that adapts to you

Every word, ayah, and surah is a card in your review queue. Get it right, the interval grows. Get it wrong, it comes back sooner. The system learns what you struggle with.

Stranger → Familiar → Known → Memorized → Mastered → Preserved

4 hours → 8 hours → 1 day → 2 days → 1 week → 2 weeks → 1 month → 4 months → 1 year
